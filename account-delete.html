This page explains how users of AllBackup can request account deletion and associated data deletion.

1) How to request account deletion

  1. The in-app deletion feature is available: Menu > User info > Delete account].
  2. Or email us to request deletion:
    Support Email: a.backup7799@gmail.com
    Suggested subject: [AllBackup] Account Deletion Request
    Please include:
    • The phone number used to sign up (or another identifier)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)

2) Data that will be deleted

  • Account information (e.g., phone number, user name)
  • User-generated content (posts and comments)
  • Notification token (e.g., FCM token / device identifier)

3) Data that may be retained and retention period

For legal and operational purposes (e.g., fraud prevention and service integrity), the following data may be retained for up to 90 days and then deleted automatically.

  • Minimal service usage logs
  • Minimum records for abuse prevention

Retention period: Up to 90 days (may be shorter depending on circumstances)

4) Partial deletion without deleting the account

Users may delete certain content (such as posts) within the app. However, a separate formal process to delete only part of the data without deleting the account may not be provided.

Contact: a.backup7799@gmail.com
